1. Go to your domain registrar’s DNS settings (e.g., GoDaddy, Namecheap, Hostinger).

2. Add a new TXT record with the following information:

• Record Type: TXT  
• Name (Host): _github-pages-challenge-darmansaz  
• Value: 37447d02b1ace2b2f210e0bf0af420
